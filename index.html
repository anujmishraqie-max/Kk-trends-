// ABOUT US

Thane's Trusted Cyber Hub

Welcome to KK Cyber Novelty — your all-in-one digital service centre in the heart of Kalwa, Thane. We've been serving students, professionals, and residents with fast, reliable, and affordable cyber services.

Whether you need to print an urgent document at 3 AM, browse high-speed internet, apply for a government certificate, or just relax with an online game — KK Cyber Novelty is open for you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Located conveniently at Shop 5, 11 BLDG, Anand Vihar Complex, Mafatlal Colony, Kalwa — just minutes away from the main road.
